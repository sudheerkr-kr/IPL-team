About CSK

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 2008, the team has been one of the most consistent performers in the IPL.

With a strong core, trust in experienced players, and calm leadership, CSK has qualified for the playoffs in most seasons they have played.
